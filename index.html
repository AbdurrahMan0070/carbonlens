Enterprise Strategy

The Business Case
for GreenOps.

Carbon-efficient code is cost-efficient code. CarbonLens provides engineering teams with the actionable metrics needed for incoming ESG compliance mandates and cloud cost reduction.

38%
Average Cost & Carbon Reduction
Studies show that smart architectural choices — efficient database queries, appropriate caching, and carbon-aware region selection — cut a software system's carbon footprint by 30-40%. Because you use less compute, this directly translates to a 30-40% reduction in cloud billing.
$2.1T
ESG Compliance Market by 2025
ESG reporting is becoming mandatory globally (e.g., EU's CSRD). CarbonLens gives enterprises a developer-first path to these metrics. No external consultants needed just the tools developers already use to export compliant PDF reports instantly.
